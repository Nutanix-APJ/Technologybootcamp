Technology Bootcamp PreReqs and Setup instructions.

PreReqs:

1)  AOS 5.8.1 or 5.8.2

2)  Update AHV to current version shipped with AOS 5.8.x


To stage this workshop, follow these steps:

1)  SSH into CVM

2)  Run the following command (replace nathan.cox with your email) "curl --remote-name --location https://raw.githubusercontent.com/nutanixworkshops/technologybootcamp/master/setup/bootstrap.sh && MY_EMAIL=nathan.cox sh ${_##*/}"

3)  Follow the prompts.
